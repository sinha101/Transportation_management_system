About Transportation Management System

Transportation Management System is a web-based app by which users will be able to book their bus ticket following the details of route distribution,transport details. Moreover users will have an idea of train and bus along with their transport details.

This system is going to be very useful for newly shifted people in this capital. We have built this system in order to help this people and make their new life a bit easier in this complicated city. .
